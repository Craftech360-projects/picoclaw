## Question

How long is the free trial, at what limits does it run, and how is trial-farming prevented?

## Resolution (2026-07-14, charting session)

- **Length: 1 month** from first activation (user originally floated 3 months; chose 1 — ~₹145 COGS giveaway per toy instead of ~₹435, and 3× faster conversion signal while pricing is unvalidated).
- **Limits: Family tier** (300 questions/mo, 40/day, unlimited images 25/day) — kid habituates to exactly the hero plan; conversion is "keep what you have".
- **Abuse: one trial per device MAC, ever.** Trial start is recorded permanently against the MAC at the device's FIRST-ever activation; factory reset, unbind/rebind, or resale never re-grants it. Second-hand buyers get no trial — accepted.
- Exact definition of the "first activation" trigger event is deliberately NOT decided here — see [Trial lifecycle design](005-trial-lifecycle-design.md).
