How Cheeko subscriptions work

Cheeko is the AI toy your child talks to. Every conversation costs real money to run — so the toy runs on a simple monthly plan, with the first month free on every new toy. This page walks through the whole system: the trial, the plans, and exactly what happens under the hood.

🎁 1 month free on first activation 💬 Plans measured in questions 🇮🇳 Pay by UPI Autopay 🔒 Stories & music cards always work
The parent journey

From unboxing to subscribed

The clock starts only when a real family connects the toy — never in the factory.

📦
Day 0

Unbox & connect

The parent app connects the toy to WiFi (Bluetooth setup), the toy shows a 6-digit code, and the parent enters it. That first-ever bind starts the free month — at Family-plan limits, no card needed.

💬
Days 1–22

Just play

The child asks, Cheeko answers. The app quietly shows a "free month" countdown and how many questions were used.

🔔
Days 23 · 27 · 30

Three gentle reminders

"One week left" → "3 days — pick a plan" → "trial ended". Never more than three pushes.

💳
Any time

Subscribe on the web portal

Plans are bought at pay.cheekoai.in — same Google sign-in as the app. One UPI Autopay approval, and renewals are automatic with a 24-hour heads-up before every charge.

🌤️
If they don't

The toy never bricks

Only the AI pauses. Story cards, music and downloaded content keep working — and Cheeko gently says: "Ask Mumma or Papa to check the Cheeko app so we can keep playing!"

The plans

Three sizes, one simple meter

A "question" is one exchange — the child asks, Cheeko replies. Parents see questions; an invisible daily time limit underneath keeps marathon story-loops from gaming the bucket.

Under the hood

What happens when a child says “Hi Cheeko”

Before any AI wakes up, the gateway asks one question: is this toy allowed a session right now? Flip the plan state and press play.

🧸Toy
"Hi Cheeko!"
🚪Gateway

🧮Manager API
session-verdict
🤖AI worker
LiveKit room

The verdict checks: active plan or trial? questions left this month? under today's caps? If our own server is down, the toy is allowed anyway — a Cheeko outage must never look like an unpaid bill. Mid-conversation, a session is never cut for an empty bucket; only the invisible daily time cap can end one early ("time for a break!").
